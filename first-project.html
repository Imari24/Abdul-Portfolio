INTRODUCTION

Cars24 Marketplace is a mobile application designed to guide first-time car buyers and novices in the car market through the car-buying process with ease and confidence. Our team conducted extensive research and analysis to identify the research problem and pain points faced by buyers, and used this information in the development of Car24 Mobile app that addresses these issues.

The Challenge and Problem Statement

It is well known that first-time car buyers and novices in the car market often feel overwhelmed by the car-buying process and struggle to find the right car that fits their needs, budget, maintenance preferences, often lack knowledge about financing and information necessary to make an informed decision leading to a high risk of making a poor purchase.


Additionally, the process of searching for cars, comparing prices and features, further complicates the process.

PAIN-POINTS AND CHALLENGES

Not enough information on “need to know” for a first timer.

Difficulty comparing options including prices and features.

Getting the right support for vehicle inspection.

Finding the right car that fits the budget and need.

Time consuming process to complete a car process.

Research and Data Collection

Digging deeper, we conducted Interviews with 20 individuals who had recently purchased a car, as well as 10 individuals who were in the process of searching for a car.. Also conducted was a competitive analysis to identify existing solutions in the market and how they addressed the pain points in their solution.

Based on our research and data, we found that first-time car buyers and novices in the car market needed a platform that was easy to use, comprehensive, and personalized. Users wanted an app that could guide them through the car-buying process, from identifying their needs and budget to providing information necessary to make a good buying decision.

Design Process

To address these challenges, we designed the Cars24 Marketplace app with the following features:

Purchase Wizard: To collect information about what needs and base on the entered parameter provide car recommendations
Side-by-side comparison: The app allows buyers to compare different car options side-by-side, making it easier to make informed decisions.
Easy-to-understand information: The app provides buyers with detailed information about cars, including specifications, features, and prices, in a user-friendly format that is easy to understand.
Time-saving tools: The app includes time-saving tools, such as a dealer locator and support for vehicle inspection, to streamline the car-buying process.
